Food

The magic about Vietnamese food is the balance they incorporate in the meals. The flavors are not strong, instead they are very plain and natural

The markets were full of odor of dry food, fruits and fish. Although it was strong in smell, the richness in colors and textures was what made the markets unique

Places

The most breathtaking views were in Sapa, a teritory full of rice crops that extent for several miles of mountains.

Another incredible place was Ha Long Bay, wich is full of thousands of limestone karsts

And of course the beutiful city of Hoi An, where yellow houses are covered in lanterns that give magic to the city when the sun sets

People

Some of the most extraordinary people I met on this country! When I would walk through the local restaurants an looked at peoples plates they would invite me with hand gestures to join them and eat.

The locals would share their life stories with me even if we know each other for a short time, showing how open and friendly they are

The most antique culture that I was exposed to was in Sapa, where the locals dress like they did a thousand years ago and they cultivate rice just for their family survival
