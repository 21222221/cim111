About me

Traveling has always been an important part of my life. Since a young age, it has provided me with experiences that have opened my mind and help me see the world in a different way.

This summer I jumped to South East Asia, as a volunteer and backpacker, to discover something I have never seen before. With high expectations and an open mind, my five-week adventure marked my life.

Laos

My first encounter with Laos was fantastic. Afew days in Luang Prabang gave me a taste of the culture of this city. However, it was in Sop Chem, a small village that I learned the most about Laos people and traditions.

It was here, in this 321 people vilage that I spent my time volunteering and participating in the daily life activities that these people have practice for centuries.
