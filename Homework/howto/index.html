How to make healthy cookies


Mantaining healty habbits can be difficult, especially when you want a dessert after diner.

That's why we have come up with an idea of how to enjoy what you like while taking care of your health.

Steps

  1. Add one ingredient at a time to the bowl
  2. Mix until you have an homogene mass
  3. Put in the oven for 35 minutes
  4. Wait until they are cold and move to a plate
  5. Serve with sald




We hope you enjoy the cookies and that you stay healthy in the future
